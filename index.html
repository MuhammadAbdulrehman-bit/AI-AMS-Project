Booking Process
Six Steps to a Confirmed Appointment
01
📱

Choose Your Channel

Book via the web portal, smartphone app, or IVR phone system. Walk-ins are assisted by a Facilitator at the branch.

02
🪪

Verify Identity

Enter your 13-digit CNIC. A 4-digit OTP is sent to your registered mobile number for confirmation.

03
📋

Select Service

Choose from 10 CDNS services — account opening, profit collection, withdrawals, and more.

04
🤖

AI Recommends Branch

The Geo-Smart Assistant analyzes your location and live congestion scores to suggest the best NSC near you.

05
🕐

Confirm Time Slot

Pick an available slot within official hours (9 AM – 3 PM). The Conflict Resolver ensures no double-bookings.

06
✅

Receive Confirmation

A unique Reference Number is generated and sent via SMS. Reminders are sent 24 hours and 1 hour before your slot.
